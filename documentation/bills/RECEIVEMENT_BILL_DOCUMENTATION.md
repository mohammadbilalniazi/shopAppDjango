Receivement Bill Documentation

1. Purpose

RECEIVEMENT bills represent cash or financial receipts received by an organization from another organization. They typically correspond to payments received for previous sales or loan recoveries.

2. Key Characteristics

- bill_type: RECEIVEMENT
- creator organization: recipient of cash
- receiver organization: payer organization
- cash flow effect: increases liquid cash asset for the recipient
- ledger effect: reduces the counterparty's debt to the recipient

3. Opposite Transaction

RECEIVEMENT bills are paired with PAYMENT bills for the payer organization. This pairing allows the system to maintain balanced cash movement between organizations.

4. Financial Impact

- financing cash flow increases for the recipient organization
- outstanding loans payable are reduced when cash is actually received
- net cash position improves by the payment amount

5. Thesis Relevance

RECEIVEMENT bills offer thesis examples of:
- cash receipt processing in multi-organization workflows
- synchronization between payment and receivable records
- the distinction between sales revenue and cash collection activities
- the effect of cash inflows on financial dashboard metrics
